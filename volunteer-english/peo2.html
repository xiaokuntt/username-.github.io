Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2017-06-13   source：

Hui Jiangtao, driver of the 619 Road of the North Bus Team of Tianjin Public Transport Company II, has been keen on public welfare undertakings while doing his job. Since 2006, he has used his spare time to actively participate in a variety of volunteer service activities such as caring for children with autism, helping the elderly, serving the needs of wheelchair passengers and disabled children. At the beginning of 2014, he brought the concept and experience of volunteer service into the bus, and learned from the aviation service standards, began to explore and innovate the journey of humanized car service deep processing, and carefully built the first class in the bus. In November of the same year, he and passengers and social caring people prepared to set up the “Tianjin Bus First Class Red Ten Volunteer Service Team” to extend personal volunteer service to group volunteer service, which was widely recognized and praised by beneficiary passengers and people with disabilities. He has won a series of honors such as the Tianjin May 1st Labor Medal, Tianjin Volunteer Sunshine Angel, and the most beautiful volunteers in Hexi District.

First, bring the concept of volunteer service into the bus, create a first class bus, and provide caring and passenger services to passengers.

More than 500,000 people with disabilities in Tianjin, because there are no barrier-free buses designed for disabled wheelchairs, this has made many disabled wheelchair users who are inconvenient to move out of the bus. Since late April 2014, the bus group has started to operate barrier-free buses in 100 bus lines in urban areas. As the driver of the first batch of barrier-free vehicles, Hui Jiangtao devoted himself to research and began to improve the service details. Starting from reducing the waiting time for wheelchair passengers and travel services, and turning passive services into active services, Hui Jiangtao printed the first disabled passenger reservation service card for Tianjin Public Transport, creating a precedent for buses to reserve services. To analyze the travel needs of disabled wheelchair passengers, and launch the exclusive project of “Blue Warm, Sunshine Helping Disabled Volunteer Service”. According to the needs of disabled wheelchair users, the volunteer service is divided into on-off help and home help. In the large category, the service radiates 38 stations in four urban areas along the route. For the disabled wheelchair passengers who need to get on and off the bus, they will issue an appointment service card, implement advance reservation, set the scheduled on-time bus service, and issue the appointment service card for the whole year. More than a thousand, booking more than 300 people；For those who need to go to the home to provide disabled wheelchairs, Hui Jiangtao will contact the members of the nearest service team to help the disabled wheelchair users who have come to the door to help them. Since the service was launched, they have helped hundreds of people with disabilities in the past two years. Xiao Zhao, who lives in the Dongli of the Dinglinyuan of Wangding, is such a helper. Xiao Zhao is 23 years old. He can't stand on his legs. He usually relies on wheelchairs. He said: "Since returning to Master to help him to travel by barrier-free bus, his travel is much more convenient, especially the return of the master to my help is really too big. Because it is not convenient to travel by wheelchair, so I used to I rarely go out in peacetime. An occasional chance, let me listen to the radio and say that the barrier-free bus that the master is driving can also make an appointment. Out of curiosity, I reported on the idea of making a phone call and started my first appointment. I didn't expect to return to Master's appointment to make a reservation. Every time he pushed me down and told me the time to return, otherwise I really don't know how to go home.From then on, every time Xiao Zhao travels, he will send back to the master to send an appointment to WeChat in advance. The returning masters and volunteers will also arrive at the station on time; truly expand the volunteer service from the car to the car, and continue to expand the coverage of the disabled. The humanized appointment service has become more and more intimate, and has been greatly recognized and praised by the disabled wheelchair passengers and the public. Everyone has sent back to Jiang Tao’s disability appointment service information through WeChat and Weibo. Many media reports have exceeded 100. Article, the current "blue warm, sunny help" brand of service projects have quietly spread throughout the beautiful Jincheng.

Second, the concept of volunteer care and dedication is sent to the people, love to build the first class of public transport, and extend the dedication from the carriage to the society.

In order to meet the needs of different passengers, and let the passengers enjoy the humanized and caring service when traveling, he carefully studied the internal structure of the barrier-free car, and used the space without seats above the front wheel to purchase the small medicine box and emergency. Drugs, toy boxes and children's toys, lost and found boxes, convenient motion sickness bags, small umbrellas, bottled water and disposable paper cups are convenient for special passengers. Drawing on aviation service standards, he has launched five intimate service series, 32 humanized service initiatives and ten service commitments for the elderly, children, exclusive, sharing and publicity, and self-taught mute, summed up the first class, welcome you, A good voice, a smile, and a set of mute services with equal features, have been praised by the majority of passengers and deaf friends. For the special passengers, he summed up a set of "one look, two support, three questions, four" work method, if you see the special passengers on the train, he will take the initiative to help him find a seat, ask where to get off And wait until the bus stops and then leave the seat. After the special passengers get off the bus, he will stop for a while and see the old man from the outside safety mirror and drive again. In the first class of the bus, you will smell a touch of jasmine tea.Experience a 30-kilometer one-way clean without a piece of paper, even if you stay for a few minutes, you will feel comfortable and warm. There are cotton pads in winter, cool pads in summer, thirsty water, sick medicine, can read newspapers, can charge mobile phones, and breastfeeding specials for breastfeeding mothers, with the atmosphere of the carriages coming The warmer the service, the more intimate service, the many passengers and caring citizens were moved, and they established contact with Master Jiang Tao and were willing to participate in volunteer service with the returning master. In November 2014, Hui Jiangtao established the “Tianjin Bus First Class with the same car”. The Volunteer Volunteer Service Team has grown from seven or eight people at the beginning of its establishment to more than 100 people. It has led the team members to participate in charity activities, extending love from the train to the society, and delivering the dream of volunteering with practical actions. Energy, they together buy books and toys for left-behind children in Sun Village, and actively participate in the adoption of love tree activities. Under the advocacy of Hui Jiangtao, a number of public transport volunteers and social lovers purchased love vegetables together to help children with leukemia, in order to provide more hope for leukemia patients.Master Jiang Jiangtao contacted a number of bus star drivers to come to the Tianjin Red Cross Society to donate hematopoietic stem cells, register them in the Chinese stem cell bank, and go deep into the community, bus stations, and bustling streets with the volunteers of the team. Leukemia patients have made a lot of effort and donated a love donation hematopoietic stem cell volunteer activity, which has aroused strong resonance and strong support from all walks of life. Under the leadership of Master Jiang Tao, the party members of the team jointly helped Wan Haixiang, a child with cerebral sequelae from Anhui to Tianjin, to visit home regularly, send rice, noodles, oil, eggs and other condolences, returning to Jiang Tao’s master. Also insist on purchasing a one-hundred-yuan bus card at their own expense to reduce their living expenses. Especially at the initiative and careful planning of Hui Jiangtao, at 12 noon on December 28, 2015, from Tianjin BusNearly 20 volunteers from all walks of life, such as Tianjin Song and Dance Troupe and Tianjin Huaxia Future Children's Art Center, held a warm and memorable birthday in the “Tianjin Bus First Class” for Wan Haixiang children who had never had a birthday due to illness. The foreigner's tenth birthday theme birthday party. In the help of Wan Haixiang's medical treatment activities, through the micro-signal of the First Class Love Volunteer Service Team, the “Love Donation for Walking Power” campaign was launched, from Tianjin public transport units, bus teams, bus drivers and volunteers, and public transport. Fans and social loved ones donated a total of 15,000 yuan to meet the cost of Wan Haixiang treatment, so that love will warm up in the hearts of strangers.

In the diary of Master Jiang Jiangtao, he wrote such a passage: "Being a bus driver, it is my duty to serve passengers. It is my life's pursuit to be a volunteer, to contribute to society and to help others. I am willing to devote my limited life to the unlimited contribution of people's volunteer service to help more people in need."
