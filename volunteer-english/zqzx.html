The second executive director of the fourth session of the association (expansion) will be held
On December 2nd, the second executive director (expansion) meeting of the 4th Council of China Youth Volunteers Association was held in Chongqing.
  • Minutes of the third meeting of the Theoretical Research Working Committee
  • Resolution of the Fourth Council on Further Strengthening Self-Building
The China Youth Volunteers Association was established on December 5, 1994. It is under the guidance of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China. It is formed by the municipal youth volunteer organizations.
[detailed]
The highest authority of the association is the member representative assembly. The member representative assembly is held every four years. The board of directors is the executive body of the member representative assembly. It leads the local association to carry out work during the intersessional period and is responsible to the member representative assembly. [detailed]
President: Wang Hongyan; Vice President: Shen Zhaojun, Bai Yansong, Liu Zhengyu, Xu Xiaoyan, Yang Yang, Yang Song, Yan Lihua, Hou Baosen, Xu Benxi, Xie Haishan, Tan Jianguang [detailed]
