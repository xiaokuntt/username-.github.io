The first working seminar of the theoretical research work committee was held in Guangzhou

Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2015-07-17   source：China Youth Network

On December 3, the first working seminar of the Theoretical Research Working Committee of the China Youth Volunteers Association was held in Guangzhou. The meeting was organized by Vice President Tan Jianguang and Vice Chairman Lian Si. Huai Yu, Wang Weizheng, Zhang Qiang, Xing Wenyi, Duan Xia, Zeng Songting, Liu Zuo, Zhang Jiaxin, Qi Fang, Wang Zhi, Li Jian, Guo Yunfang, Zhao Hongliang, Huang Guangying, Li Wei, Shi Shuai attended the meeting。

　　After several hours of heated discussion, the committee members made three suggestions.

　 　First, we must carry out theoretical research on volunteer service, pay extensive attention to the history and development of international and domestic voluntary service development, organize and coordinate to assist in the basic theory and applied research of volunteer service, and refine the voluntary service model with typical promotion significance in the country. Provide reference for policy development of volunteer services.

　　The second is to build a theoretical exchange platform for volunteer service theory, give full play to the theoretical expertise of the committee members, publish a group of theoretical secretaries of volunteer service, compile a journal of voluntary service theory research, and organize academic conferences or training sessions on voluntary service theory research.

　　The third is to understand the annual work of the volunteers, the work of the various research committees, grasp the youth volunteer work as a whole and contact the actual research to provide theoretical support for the work of the association. The first is to contact several leaders' reports and theoretical research work committees to conduct theoretical research on volunteer service work. In the next stage of work, we will first publish the "Blue Book of Chinese Youth Volunteers", "China Volunteer Service Theory and Practice Series", research and publish "China Youth Review and Expert Volunteer Service Special Issue", and also hold the peak of Chinese volunteer service. Forum to carry out collective theoretical research and exchange work。

The committee members unanimously stated that under the leadership and guidance of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the China Youth Volunteers Association, the theoretical research work committee has the determination and confidence to push forward the theoretical research work of the Chinese youth volunteer service and provide support for the work of volunteer service. Strong theoretical support.
