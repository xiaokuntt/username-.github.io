The third meeting of the Cultural Propaganda Working Committee was held
In order to co-ordinate the media concentration work during the Chongqing Zhizhi Fair and comprehensively enhance the visibility and influence of the competition, on the afternoon of November 23, the China Youth Volunteers Association held the third work seminar of the Cultural Propaganda Working Committee in the conference room of the Central Committee of the Youth League. The special study will be held on the second China Youth Volunteer Service Project Competition and Chongqing Zhizhi Fair and the 2016 Volunteer Service Key Project. Yang Song, the director of the Youth Volunteer Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Lu Xiaohua, Yan Mingmei, the deputy director of the Cultural Propaganda Work Committee of the China Youth Volunteers Association, and more than 20 representatives from the Beijing Committee attended the meeting.
