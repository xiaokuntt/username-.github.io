Visit to the nursing home

Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2015-06-03     source： Guidian Dandelion Love Society

&We will organize classmates to enroll in the nursing home in the school, go to the nursing home with us, tell their grandmother and grandmother about their stories, talk about their past, listen to their voices, and let every volunteer come to them. Very happy, we also learned from their mouths about life decades ago and learned some philosophies from their conversations. I hope that you can join us and walk into the hearts of the old people.

Can you help these grandfathers and grandmothers realize their dreams?

It is the law of nature to live and die. One day, we will grow old. One day, we will have a full story to find someone to talk to, share, and talk about the people and things we have encountered in this life. So I hope we can spend more time with these grandfathers and grandfathers and listen to their stories.
Have love with us！
