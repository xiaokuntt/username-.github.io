Social volunteer service system

Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2015-07-15   source：China Youth Network

      　

     Content recommendation：

　 　Social Volunteer Service System - China Volunteer Service“Guangdong experience”" is a book that tries to answer the above questions.“Guangdong experience”Based on rich practical experience and long-term theoretical research. The two authors in Guangdong tried to summarize and analyze the elements, rational patterns and construction approaches of the social volunteer service system, especially in combination with the exploration experience accumulated in various places, especially in Guangdong, and to answer the key questions and countermeasures in the construction of this system. This is a systematic attempt that is still rare in this respect. It is believed that it also contains many inspiring understandings, which will definitely play a positive and valuable role in the improvement and development of China's social volunteer service system. I hope that more people of insight will pay more attention to this topic and jointly promote the country's volunteer service industry to a new stage of development.
