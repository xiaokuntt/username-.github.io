"Love Angel" for volunteer service

Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2017-06-13   source：

There is always a sweet smile on her face. She wades through the mountains only to send warmth to those in need; in the face of helpless people in trouble, she did her best to lend a helping hand. At the age of 22, she just graduated from college, but she has been on the road of volunteering for nearly 6 years, and her personal volunteer service lasted nearly 4,000 hours. She is the captain of the Rainbow Youth Volunteer Service Team in Yunxiao County and Lin Min, the deputy head of the County Youth Volunteer Service Brigade.

In June 2011, Lin Min, a high school student at the time, contacted more than a dozen teachers and students and initiated the establishment of the Yunxiao County Rainbow Volunteer Service Team. In the past few years, she has led the volunteer team to develop more than 500 young volunteers. She has organized “helping orphans and swallows sisters”, “helping seriously ill students Xiaohaoze”, “donating clothes for the northwest region”, “recruiting the community for first aid”, and “youth”. 350 times of various volunteer activities such as “Love for Public Welfare Activities”, “Caring for Autistic Family”, and “Community Youth League Compulsory Saturday”, and assisting 2516 people, including orphans, poor students, seriously ill students, disabled people, elderly widows, and empty nesters. Volunteer service time is more than 45,000 hours, and more than 1.2 million yuan of love money is distributed. At the same time, more than 30 first-aid training and safety education were carried out, benefiting nearly 10,000 students and teachers, and saving more than 400,000 yuan in training fees. The Volunteer Team has also been the summer camp for cross-strait youths, the “Industrial Disabled Art Troupe into the Yunyi Fair”, the “Da Xing Yunxiao Spring Festival Evening”, “2016 Zhangzhou Xueleifeng Day Theme Activity and Youth Poverty Alleviation”, “The Seventh Cross-Strait More than 40 large-scale events, such as the Kailuan King Culture Festival, provide a variety of volunteer services such as comprehensive logistics support, with a service duration of nearly 10,000 hours.

When building the team, the funds were tight and there was no transportation. Sometimes, in order to save money, Lin Min often took the team to bring the condolences and equipment to walk nearly 5 kilometers to the mountain village to visit the orphans. In June 2011, the “Swallows Sisters” of the poverty-stricken villages in Fengwai Village lost their parents and faced the desperate situation in life. Lin Min led the volunteer team to raise more than 40,000 yuan for them and rekindled the fire of hope. In November 2011, the orphans and poor students of Shuangling Village suffered from serious illness and their lives were at stake. Lin Min led the volunteer team to run around. In just over a month, they raised more than 120,000 yuan of donations, all of which were used to treat Xiao Haize, to restore their health and complete their studies. From 2012 to 2016, on New Year's Eve, she led volunteers to go to the remote mountain villages to accompany the orphans and disabled children and the elderly. Around the Spring Festival in 2015 and during the summer, the volunteer team was invited to condolence to nearly 200 families, and donated 50,000 yuan of love and money; assisted the Yunxiao Red Cross to distribute 600,000 yuan of love materials to benefit 1,500 families with difficult families. In 2016, we assisted the provincial and municipal committees at all levels to carry out public welfare activities and volunteer services, and distributed nearly 600,000 yuan of love and money.

Under her guidance and leadership, the energetic volunteers are like a drop of water, gathered together, united, merged into a spring, dedicated to love, and serve the people in need: let the children who are out of school reappear The smile, into the yearning campus; let the lonely old man feel the warmth of the society, the happiness of the family; let the people struggling on the edge of death get timely treatment, get the courage to overcome the disease... Lin Min and the aided orphans, left-behind children, people in need, and volunteers formed a deep friendship and were called "Little White Sister" intimately.

She led the volunteer team to professionally manage and standardize operations, and successfully built a number of youth charity projects. Among them, "Children's Rainbow Bridge: Caring for Orphans and Protecting Growth Public Welfare Project" has been carried out since the establishment of the team in 2011. It provides visitor condolences, difficult assistance, academic counseling, family companionship, society for 100 orphans and unaccompanied children in Yunxiao Township. Volunteer services such as experience, self-care education, rehabilitation medicine, psychological counseling, etc., help solve the outstanding problems of the aided groups such as “family loss, psychological imbalance, behavior out of control, learning failure, rehabilitation, safety, and guardianship”. Guarantee the growth of the recipients with love, education, education, family, and safety. More than 100 orphans and unaccompanied children receive quality, long-term family acquaintances. In March 2013, the project was awarded the “Second Fujian Xuelei Feng Excellent Volunteer Service Project”.

The “Life Escort: Emergency Relief Training Program” was launched to benefit more than 8,000 teachers and students, and 500 people received the Red Cross Primary Responders. "Rainbow Plan: Care for the Difficult Minors to Hand in Action" to help more than 300 minors. In October 2014, the “Rainbow Project” project was listed as the “Cultivating Brand Volunteer Service Team Funding Project” in 2014 by the Provincial Committee Civilization Office. The Volunteer Team also launched the “Qingyi Help Fly: Public Welfare Volunteer Training Incubation Center” to provide training courses for volunteers and non-profit organizations in the county, providing services such as project cultivation, team incubation, and resource docking. In January 2016, the “Blue Ribbon Action: Autistic Children's Family Mutual Aid Alliance” project was launched to provide volunteer services for children and their families between 1 and 16 years old in Yunxiao County, directly benefiting 150 people. The project is dedicated to “moving institutions into the family” and making parents the best healers. The project was shortlisted in the third National Youth Volunteer Service Competition in September 2016.

The Volunteer Team carried out the "Clover" single family care campaign, and participated in 33 volunteers in the urban and rural areas of Yunxiao County to carry out voluntary service and social work, and used psychological intervention techniques such as psychological interview, psychological support, emotional support, and mutual assistance to help the disabled families. Repair physical and mental trauma and rebuild confidence in life. Autistic children and their families from 1 to 16 years old in Yunxiao County provide volunteer services and directly benefit from 150 people. The project is dedicated to “moving institutions into the family” and making parents the best healers. The project was shortlisted in the third National Youth Volunteer Service Competition in September 2016.

In cooperation with other communities such as Shuyang and other cities, we will carry out the “Good Side” program: Neighborhood Watching Volunteer Service, to “help the poor to send warmth, help the doctor to send health, help the music to send entertainment, help to send comfort, help students to send knowledge, "Helping the rescue, helping the law" is the main content, focusing on the empty nest elderly, left-behind children, migrant workers and disabled people, as the guardian of the neighbors, the intimate people of the empty nest, and the escorts of the children.

In August 2014, Lin Min was named “2013 National Figure Person of the Year”; in December 2014, he was awarded “Chuzhou Volunteer Dedication to Youth”, and in March 2015 and March 2016, he was awarded “Chuzhou Youth Volunteer” twice. Excellent individual award for service action; in November 2015, he was named "Chuzhou Excellent Young Pioneers Volunteer Counselor". In May 2016, he was awarded the “2015 Top Ten Communist Youth League Member of Fujian Province”.

Colorful Rainbow Volunteer Team has won the "Zhuzhou City Volunteer Service Excellent Organization", "Cangzhou Youth Volunteer Excellent Organization Award", "Fujian Province's Best Volunteer Service Organization", "The 13th Fujian Province May Fourth Youth Medal Collective" More than 50 provincial and municipal awards. In 2015 and 2016, the Colorful Volunteer Team Charity Project participated in the China Charity Project Exchange Exhibition twice. A number of activity cases were exchanged in the China Civilization Network and the Chinese Community Network, and were included in the Provincial Volunteer Service Series "Warming Meet". The results of activities such as "Love to Set Up a Rainbow Bridge in the Neighborhood of Rainbow Bridge" were promoted in the China Civilization Network and selected as the first anniversary of the volunteer activities of the Chinese community neighborhood watch.
