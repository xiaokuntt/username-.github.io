Volunteer: Wang Fengjin

Guidian Volunteer Network     date：2017-10-19    source：China Volunteer Service Network

Wang Fengjin, female, born in May 1958, the capital's Shi Leifeng team.

On October 15, 2007, Wang Fengjin listened to the report of the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and saw that the motherland was growing prosperously. The idea of setting up a Leifeng team was born in the heart and was supported by Yinshan Rental Company. After Wang Fengjin's intense preparations, he absorbed the unit "Taxi Star" and party members. The capital's Shi Leifeng team was formally established on the morning of December 29, 2007, and Wang Fengjin was the captain. She insisted on self-study in foreign languages and mastered the basic exchanges of six national languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Thai, German, and Vietnamese. Her guests are Chinese, foreigners, Beijingers, and foreigners. No matter who they are, she treats guests like family members.

In August 2010, after seeing reports of disabled people getting to the car, Wang Fengjin announced a personal phone call. Only herself, she was free to pick up more than 800 elderly and physically disabled passengers. Usually, every time you pick up a passenger, you put a piece of money into the "love oil barrel". For more than 10 years, you have donated 50,000 yuan. Under her leadership, the “Capital Lei Shifeng Team” has grown and expanded. The number of players has increased from the initial 25 to 220. The number of convenience service projects has increased from several to 20, and it is shuttled daily in the streets of Beijing. Extend warm hands to those passengers and people who have difficulties, and let Lei Feng’s ruts stretch in the capital. "Lei Feng Racing Team" also formed a joint construction and assistance with Beijing Leifeng Primary School, Haidian Disabled Persons' Federation, Fengtai Senior Citizens' Home and other units, and became a veritable "party branch on the wheel."

Since 2012, under her advocacy, the “National Leifeng Team Major League” has been established. In Tianjin, Shanghai, Changsha, Zhengzhou, Hangzhou and Lanzhou, 120 “China Leifeng Teams” have been established, covering 26 provinces and cities nationwide. A total of more than 50,000 people. She won the top ten taxi heroes of the 3rd "China Petroleum Beijing Cup", the top ten stars of the "People's Love Story" in Beijing, the Capital Spiritual Civilization Construction Medal, the Beijing March Eighth Red Flag Medal, the Capital May 1st Labor Medal, and the National Five A labor medal, the capital "Lei Feng · the most beautiful Beijingers" pacesetter, the fourth National Moral Model Nomination Award.
